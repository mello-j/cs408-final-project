Transactions

Welcome to our Transactions page!

  1. Here you can add new items to the database. You can add items one at a time or in bulk.
  2. To add items one at a time, fill out the form below and click the "Add Item" button.
  3. To add items in bulk, click the "Multiple Entry Mode" button and fill out the table that appears.
  4. To submit your data, click the "Add Transaction" button.
  5. Made a mistake? No worries, click the Ooopsie button to filter data and delete or edit as needed!
